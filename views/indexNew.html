Discover who is tracking you online

Collusion is an add-on for Firefox and allows you to see all the third parties that are tracking your movements across the Web. It will show, in real time, the interaction between companies and other trackers with various visualizaitons.


MAKING THE INVISIBLE VISIBLE

Explore the Database

Browse first and third-party connection data uploaded by anonymous data donors that use the Collusion add-on. W're building this crowd-sourced directory to encourage users to visualize the state of tracking on the web as an aggregate and see relationships that exist on the web. >> Go to Database

------------------------- HOW COLLUSION WORKS -------------------------

EXPLORE VISUALIZATION

Download the add-on to your Firefox broswer.
Open the add-on to see visualizations of your browsing being tracked by third parties.

ANALYZE THE TRACKERS

As you browse online, the visualizations update in real time generating your data into various views you choose.

CONTRIBUTE DATA

Explore and discover trackers and you can save your data as a .json file to make your own visualizations or donate your data to our database.

Tracking & Privacy

Not all tracking is bad. Many services rely on user data to provide relevant content and enhance your online experience. But most tracking happens without users' consent and without their knowledge. That's not okay. It should be you who decides when , how and if you want to be tracked. We recognize the importance of transparency and our mission is all about empowering users -- both with tools and with information. >> Go to About
